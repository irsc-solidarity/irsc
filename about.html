Our Vision

A harmonious India and world where people of all faiths and beliefs are free to practice.

Living in mutual respect, understanding, and shared commitment to peace, justice, and human dignity.

Our Mission

Meaningful Dialogue

Promote mutual understanding between diverse religions, faith, and spiritual traditions.

Religious Diversity

Promote cultural and religious diversity and stand up against extremism and supremacist ideas.

Promote Spirituality

Promote values of love, empathy, and solidarity, and discourage mindless consumerism.

Social Solidarity

Collaborate to undertake solidarity actions and socially relevant services to serve the needy.

Building Bridges

Work towards building bridges through education, advocacy, and joint action in Mumbai communities.

Moral Clarity

Convene interfaith dialogues to explore ethical and spiritual resources for peace.

Why We Exist

The Rationale

Given the present sociopolitical context—marked by increasing extremist expressions and assertions of religious supremacy—there is an urgent need to revive and amplify the ethical teachings of various religious traditions that emphasize coexistence and empathy.

Religion can be a source to guide human conscience towards compassion, forgiveness, truth, and nonviolence, acting as a bridge between different communities.

Background & Impact

Since its inception, the IRSC has convened thematic discussions like the Mumbai Parliament of Religion , commemorating the historic address of Swami Vivekananda.
